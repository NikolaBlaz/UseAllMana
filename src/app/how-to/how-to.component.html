How to Play the Game

  1. 1. Each round you are given 5 cards with differing mana costs
  2. 2. The objective of the game is to pick cards in the correct order starting from least to most expensive (mana cost)
  3. 3. You can skip a round if you can not complete it (pressing Spacebar or clicking NEXT), but each skip will lower your score by 1 point + the number of total skips
  4. 4. The game ends when the timer reaches 0 seconds
